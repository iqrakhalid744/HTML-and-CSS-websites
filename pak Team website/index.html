    • Pakistan Cricket Team

    Introduction

    The Pakistan national cricket team, established in 1952, has a rich and vibrant history in international cricket. Known for its passionate fan base and a flair for the dramatic, the team has produced numerous cricketing legends, including Imran Khan, Wasim Akram, and Shahid Afridi.

    History

    Pakistan's cricket journey is marked by significant achievements, including winning the ICC Cricket World Cup in 1992, where Imran Khan led the team to victory against England. They also claimed the ICC Champions Trophy in 2017, showcasing their prowess in the limited-overs format

    Achievements

    In Test cricket, Pakistan has consistently been competitive, with notable series wins against top cricketing nations. The team has also produced remarkable performances in T20 cricket, winning the ICC T20 World Cup in 2009.
