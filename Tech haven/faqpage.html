    1. Why does the phone heat up obviously in summer?

    The main causes is Ambient Temperature, your phone will activate the corresponding performance scheduling method as soon as it reaches the temperature control point, which is determined by the surface temperature of the device. To guarantee a regular and consistent experience.

    2. How to Fix the Problem of Syncing error

    There are numerous steps you can take to address problems with syncing. First, ensure that you are connected to the Internet, and that the service you are trying to sync with, such as Google or Dropbox, isn't down. Double check that your password is correct and try syncing again. Still having problems? Remove the account from your device and add it again.

    3. Some Quick Fixes For Connectivity Issues?

    If you are having a problem connecting to Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or your cellular network, enable Airplane mode for 30 seconds, toggle it off, and try connecting again.
